About

Formed in Mesa, Arizona in 1993, Jimmy Eat World emerged from the underground emo scene to become one of the most influential alternative rock bands of the 2000s. With their breakthrough album "Bleed American", they brought emo into the mainstream while never losing their authentic edge.
